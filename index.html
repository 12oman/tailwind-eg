About This Project

This case study explores the integration of artificial intelligence (AI) tools with the creative coding framework p5.js to enhance student learning through the creation of a digital artwork. Adopting the PAIR (Problem formulation, AI tool selection, Interaction, Reflection) model, we designed a series of activities for students to incorporate AI into their creative coding projects (Oguz A. Acar, 2023). The activities guided students through problem formulation, AI tool selection, interaction with the AI to generate code and refine their artwork, and reflection on the experience. Student and teacher reflections provided insights into the challenges and potential of merging AI with creative coding. The case study highlights the innovative possibilities at the intersection of AI and art and offers a structured approach for educators to engage students in AI-enhanced creative projects.
